// how_it_works

Audit. Optimize. Publish.

Three steps from invisible to agent-native. Takes minutes, not months.

01
Audit
CEO scans your product and returns a CEO score — 0 to 100. Every gap mapped: missing llms.txt, no capability schema, unregistered APIs, low Claw Engine rank.
02
Optimize
CEO auto-generates your llms.txt, builds your capability manifest, maps your existing APIs to A2A-compatible action endpoints. All in your brand voice.
03
Publish
Your profile is published to the Claw Engine registry. OpenClaw agents start finding and using you. Analytics show you exactly who, when, and for what.
