Process

Audit. Optimize. Publish.

Three steps from invisible to agent-native. Takes minutes, not months.

01
Audit
CEO scans your product and returns a CEO score — 0 to 100. Every gap mapped: missing llms.txt, no capability schema, unregistered in the Claw Engine.
$ claw scan --url your.com
02
Optimize
Auto-generates your llms.txt, builds your capability manifest, maps your APIs to A2A action endpoints — one command, zero dev work.
$ claw optimize --auto
03
Publish
Published to the Claw Engine registry. Agents start finding you. Analytics show who, when, and for what — real time.
$ claw publish --registry
